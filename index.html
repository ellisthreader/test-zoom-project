Questions about AI virtual agents.

Yes. The demo models CRM, helpdesk, billing, knowledge-base, and telephony connections.

Can it handle voice calls?

Yes. The voice workflow includes live transcripts, turn timing, spoken responses, and escalation.

How do teams prevent risky actions?

Sensitive workflows use policy checks, approval queues, human handoff, and audit trails.

How do we know when the agent is ready?
