# Project Notes

## Most Important Note: ChatoraAi Product Direction

This project should present **ChatoraAi** as the user's own software product.
It should not read like a generic portfolio demo or a job-description checklist.

ChatoraAi is a voice agent deployment platform that helps teams:

- Set up customer deployment workspaces.
- Connect CRM, telephony, knowledge-base, and helpdesk systems.
- Tune voice behavior before launch.
- Run scripted and adversarial evaluation gates.
- Capture deployment insights and product gaps.
- Generate pilot-to-production handoff reports.

Brand requirements:

- Use the exact product name `ChatoraAi`.
- Use a credible B2B company design language.
- Avoid overused purple AI gradients, decorative blobs, and vague AI visuals.
- Keep the interface clean, sectioned, and focused on showing the software workflow.
- Use `assets/chatoraai-logo.svg` as the product logo.

## Target Job Description Context

This project is being shaped around the following role:

**Applied AI Engineer**  
**Job ID:** R19227  
**Location:** London, United Kingdom / Remote, United Kingdom  
**Team:** Product  
**Type:** Full time  

### What You Can Expect

Deploy production AI agents for strategic customers during the sales cycle, taking Zoom Virtual Agent from proof-of-concept to live, trusted daily use within 90 days. Partner across Sales, Product, and Engineering to prove technical value, working hands-on with voice AI, integrations, and real customer data. Turn field insights into platform capabilities that scale, ensuring every deployment makes the next one faster. The work directly accelerates revenue and shapes the product roadmap.

### About The Team

The team deploys Zoom Virtual Agent into live customer environments. It bridges Sales and Engineering through hands-on technical validation and exists to convert pilots into production systems.

### Responsibilities

- Design and deploy production-ready AI agents during proof-of-concept engagements, integrating with customer CRMs, telephony systems, and knowledge bases using Python and TypeScript.
- Engineer voice experiences by tuning speech synthesis, turn-taking, and barge-in across providers to match customer requirements and call profiles.
- Build evaluation frameworks with scripted tasks and adversarial scenarios that measure resolution rate, containment, and customer satisfaction as deployment gates.
- Capture product gaps and contribute structured feedback to Engineering, generalising field solutions into reusable platform capabilities.
- Transition successful pilots to Professional Services or partners with clear documentation, playbooks, and recommendations for ongoing operation.

### What They Are Looking For

- 4+ years in customer-facing technical roles shipping complex SaaS or AI/ML solutions, such as Solutions Engineering, Technical Account Management, or similar.
- Experience deploying LLM-based agents with tool use, multi-step orchestration, and guardrails into production customer environments.
- Voice stack expertise across ASR, TTS, turn-taking, and barge-in, including tuning at least one major provider such as ElevenLabs, Azure, Cartesia, or similar.
- Ability to build production integrations in Python with working knowledge of TypeScript or Go, connecting enterprise systems such as CRMs and telephony platforms.
- Ability to write tests, log failures, and iterate against measurable targets to validate agent performance before go-live.
- Clear, confident communication of technical concepts to business and engineering stakeholders.
- Contact centre domain knowledge, such as Genesys, Five9, NICE, Zoom Contact Center, or experience with AI agent platforms such as Decagon, Sierra, or similar.
- Contributions to voice agent benchmarks, open-source projects, or experience deploying in regulated industries such as healthcare, financial services, or telecom.
